01 — Field Notes

About the Observer

I’m Jatin Kumar Mehta — a final-year B.Tech CSE student at ABSS Institute of Technology, Meerut (AKTU, Lucknow), graduating in 2026. I work at the intersection of data, artificial intelligence, and real-world systems.

What drives me isn’t just the code — it’s the questions underneath it. Why does a model fail at the edge? What patterns are hiding in the noise? How do you build something that genuinely helps people? Those are the problems I wake up thinking about.

I’m equally captivated by space exploration, theoretical physics, and pure mathematics — disciplines that taught me to sit with hard problems, trust the process, and find beauty in emergent structure.

I founded and chaired Codoko Club at ABSS — the institute’s first coding club — for two years. I led my team to 4th place out of 100+ teams at a state hackathon. Currently building Healzenic, a wellness monitoring platform, independently.
